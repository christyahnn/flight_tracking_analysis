flights_2024.csv – Sample dataset of U.S. commercial flights in 2024, including airline, airport codes, departure and arrival times, delay information, and cancellation indicators.

airports.csv – Dataset containing latitude, longitude, and metadata for all U.S. commercial airports, used for mapping and geographic analysis.

report.Rmd – R Markdown file performing exploratory data analysis (EDA) on flight delays and cancellations, including visualizations by airline, airport, route, time, and geography.

report.md - md presentation of results 

AirPassengers.csv - Dataset containing the year, month, and # of passengers travelling domestically and internationally, used for tracking seasonal travelling patterns across years. (taken from the US Department of Transportation; Bureau of Transportation Statistics)
